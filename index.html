Welcome to Scale, a website that helps you decide how picky you should be when selecting partners.

First, you need to know how many people you're selecting from, Wolfram Alpha can be helpful here.

Then, for each of the qualities below, decide what range you'd be willing to accept in a partner.

At the bottom of the page you'll find how many people exist that you'd be happy with.
